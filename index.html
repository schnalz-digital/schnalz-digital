Our Work
Proven Global Success

Bullish (NYSE: BLSH)

Schnalz supported Bullish, a regulated and licensed digital asset exchange for institutions, in its global brand positioning and digital marketing strategy during its launch. The company successfully raised USD 10 billion and achieved profitability within just 6 months, establishing itself as a trusted leader in institutional digital asset trading.

Block.one & EOS.IO

Through strategic communications and marketing campaigns, Schnalz helped Block.one and EOS.IO build global awareness for their blockchain ecosystem. This contributed to one of the largest ICOs in history, raising over USD 4 billion via the EOS blockchain, positioning EOS.IO as a leading decentralized platform.

Cristel France

Schnalz partnered with Cristel France to enhance its luxury cookware brand presence online. By optimizing digital campaigns and strengthening SEO visibility, Cristel expanded its reach into new international markets and increased online sales by double‑digit percentages year‑over‑year.

Expansys (LON: XPS)

For Expansys, a global consumer electronics retailer, Schnalz implemented targeted performance marketing and e‑commerce optimization strategies. These efforts boosted organic traffic, improved conversion rates, and strengthened Expansys’ competitive positioning in the European and Asian markets.

Our portfolio demonstrates how Schnalz drives measurable impact for both startups and global enterprises. Learn more about us to discover our story and expertise.

About Schnalz
Marketing Agency
